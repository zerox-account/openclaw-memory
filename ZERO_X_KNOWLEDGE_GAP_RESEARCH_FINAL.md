## 5. Biomass Supply Chain
**File:** `biomass_supply_chain.md`  
**Status:** ✅ Complete (2m 26s)

### Key Findings

**Global Feedstock Availability:**

| Feedstock | Global Volume | Collection Cost | Best Regions |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|--------------|
| Agricultural residues | 450-950 Mt/year | $8-25/tonne | Asia, Americas |
| Forestry waste | 150-300 Mt/year | $15-35/tonne | Nordics, Canada |
| Urban wood waste | 50-100 Mt/year | $20-45/tonne | EU, US cities |
| Energy crops | 10-50 Mt/year | $30-60/tonne | EU, UK |

**Transportation Economics:**

| Mode | Cost | Best For | Optimal Distance |
|------|------|----------|------------------|
| Truck | $0.08-0.35/tonne-km | Flexibility | 50-80 km |
| Rail | $0.03-0.08/tonne-km | Bulk | 200-500 km |
| Barge | $0.02-0.05/tonne-km | Rivers | 300-800 km |

**Energy Density Impact:**
- Raw wood chips: 150-200 kg/m³
- Pellets: 600-700 kg/m³ (**3-4x denser**)
- Transport cost per MWh: Pellets 60% cheaper

**Supply Chain Models:**

| Model | Cost Impact | Control | Best For |
|-------|-------------|---------|----------|
| Hub-and-spoke | **15-25% savings** | Medium | Established markets |
| Direct from source | Baseline | High | Large single source |
| Third-party supplier | +10-20% premium | Low | New markets |
| Vertical integration | Best long-term | Full | Strategic markets |

**X-150 Feedstock Specifications:**
- Moisture: <20% (optimal 10-15%)
- Size: 10-50 mm
- Contamination: <1% (no metals/stones)
- Ash content: <15%

**Cost Models for X-150:**

| Scenario | Cost/tonne | Annual Cost (700t) | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Base case** | $102 | $71,400 | Third-party supplier |
| **Best case** | $75 | $52,500 | Own collection, hub-and-spoke |
| **Worst case** | $135 | $94,500 | Long transport, premium feedstock |

**Storage Requirements:**
- Covered storage: $1-5/tonne/month
- Outdoor storage: $0.50-2/tonne/month (weather risk)
- Fire suppression: Required for >100t storage
- Inventory: 2-4 weeks recommended

### Strategic Recommendations
- **Immediate:** Establish hub-and-spoke in target markets
- **Feedstock mix:** 70% primary + 30% backup sources
- **Contracts:** 1-3 year supply agreements with price caps
- **Quality control:** On-site moisture testing, pre-processing
