   - Timeline: 6-12 months
   - ROI: Immediate access to €140-160/tonne pricing
   - NPV of certification: €500K-1M over project life

2. **Target US Projects for 45Q**
   - Additional $85/tonne for 12 years
   - Combined with Puro.earth for maximum revenue
   - Focus on states with high tipping fees (California, Northeast)

3. **Develop Biochar Sales Channels**
   - Agricultural partnerships (soil amendment)
   - Filtration market (water treatment facilities)
   - Premium pricing for certified biochar

### Medium-Term Strategy (3-12 months)

4. **Multi-Standard Certification**
   - Add Verra VCS (broader market access)
   - Gold Standard (sustainability co-benefits)
   - EBC (European Biochar Certificate)

5. **Long-Term Offtake Agreements**
   - Target 5-10 year carbon credit contracts
   - Corporate buyers: Microsoft, Shopify, Stripe
   - Floor price mechanisms for risk mitigation

6. **Digital MRV System**
   - IoT sensors for continuous monitoring
   - Blockchain tracking for supply chain
   - Reduce verification costs by 30-50%
