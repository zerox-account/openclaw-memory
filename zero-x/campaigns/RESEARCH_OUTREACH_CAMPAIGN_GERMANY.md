## EMAIL TEMPLATE

**Subject:** Project Cométha Completion & Collaboration Opportunities - Zero-X Labs

---

Dear [Name],

I hope this message finds you well. My name is [Sender Name] from Zero-X Labs, and I am reaching out regarding our recent work in waste gasification and potential synergies with your research.

**Project Cométha Completion**

We have recently completed Project Cométha, a two-year demonstration project conducted in Paris in collaboration with CEAP and CCAF (France). The project successfully validated the conversion of:
- Sewage sludge
- Municipal solid waste (sub-10mm fraction)
- Horse manure (post-biodigester)

The process involved drying feedstock and converting it via oxy-fuel/oxy-steam gasification into syngas with high methane and hydrogen content. We also integrated water-gas shift reaction and biological methanization.

**Technical Validation**

The system achieved over 1,000 hours of continuous uptime at 15 kg dry feedstock per hour on a small commercial scale. The work was conducted in collaboration with Fraunhofer Institute and has been validated by Hochschule Zittau-Görlitz (Professor Tobias Trunke team), with final reports pending. DBFZ (Deutsche Biomasseforschungszentrum) initially brought us into this project.

**Current Development: Project Verkozo**

We are now in the final stages of Project Verkozo with Fraunhofer IKTS, where our syngas is being tested in solid oxide fuel cells (SOFC) for power generation using the complete syngas composition, not just methane fractions.

**X-150 System**

Based on these learnings, we are currently developing the X-150, a 150 kg dry feedstock per hour containerized gasification unit. We are exploring applications including combined heat and power (CHP) generation.

**Purpose of Contact**

We are seeking to build connections within the research community to:
- Learn about current work in specialized fuel processing and waste treatment
- Explore potential collaboration opportunities on follow-up projects
- Identify alignment with Horizon Europe calls and funded research programs
- Connect with industry partners who might benefit from this technology

We are not looking for immediate commercial partnerships, but rather to establish a network of peers working on similar challenges. If you are working on specialized fuels, waste treatment innovations, or have industry connections that might find this relevant, we would welcome a brief conversation.
