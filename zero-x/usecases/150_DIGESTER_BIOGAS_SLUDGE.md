# X-150 Use Case: Biogas Plant Sludge Processing (150 Digester)

**Product Name:** 150 Digester / X150-SS (Sewage Sludge)  
**Application:** Post-treatment of biogas plant digestate/sludge  
**Output:** High-methane gas for pipeline injection  
**Status:** Proven at Project Cométha (Paris)  
**Market:** Global biogas plant optimization  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-06

---

## Executive Summary

The X-150 can process sludge/digestate from existing biogas plants (anaerobic digesters) to extract additional methane. This post-treatment process uses oxygen and steam in combination with a methanization reactor to produce high-quality methane gas that can be fed back into the biogas plant's existing gas scrubbing system for pipeline injection.

**Value Proposition:** Increase biogas plant efficiency by 15-30% without major infrastructure changes.

---

## Technical Process

### Input: Biogas Plant Digestate
- **Source:** Anaerobic digestion of agricultural waste, food waste, sewage sludge
- **Current disposal:** Land application, composting, or disposal (costly)
- **Remaining energy:** 20-40% of original feedstock energy remains in digestate

### X-150 Processing
```
Digestate/Sludge
    ↓
X-150 Gasifier (oxygen + steam injection)
    ↓
Methanization Reactor
    ↓
Syngas → Methane enrichment
    ↓
High-methane gas (85-95% CH₄)
    ↓
Existing biogas plant scrubbing system
    ↓
Pipeline injection or on-site use
```

### Key Process Parameters
- **Temperature:** 800-1000°C
- **Gasification agent:** Oxygen + steam (not air)
- **Output gas methane content:** 85-95%
- **Process efficiency:** 70-80% of remaining energy recovered
