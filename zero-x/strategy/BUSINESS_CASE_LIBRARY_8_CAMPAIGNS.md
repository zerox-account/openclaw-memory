## Business Case 1: 150 IMPACT COOL — DATA CENTERS

**Campaign Name:** Project COOLSTACK  
**Tagline:** *"Turn Data Center Heat into Cooling Gold"*

### Target Profile
**Primary:** Hyperscale data centers (10-50 MW IT load)  
**Secondary:** Regional data centers (5-10 MW), Edge facilities (1-5 MW)  
**Geography:** Europe (priority), Middle East, Asia-Pacific

### The Problem
- Cooling = 40% of data center energy consumption
- PUE averages 1.67 (industry standard), Google/Meta at 1.2
- Electricity costs: €0.20-0.35/kWh
- Peak demand charges: €50K-100K/day for 13 MW cooling load
- ESG pressure: Scope 2 emissions reporting

### Zero-X Solution
**Configuration:** X-150 + Adsorption Chiller
- **Cooling output:** 150-200 kW per unit (COP 0.74)
- **Thermal input:** 550 kWth from X-150
- **PUE improvement:** 1.67 → 1.15 (industry-leading)

### Unit Economics (10 MW Facility, 19% Cooling Coverage)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Units required** | 8 X-150 + 8 chillers |
| **CapEx** | €3.246M |
| **Annual cooling savings** | €5.4M |
| **Payback** | 7.1 months |
| **10-year NPV** | €56M |
| **IRR** | 124% |

### Contract Structure
- **15-year PPA** for cooling services
- **Take-or-pay:** Minimum 80% capacity payment
- **Escalator:** 2.5% annually
- **Payment:** €0.04/kWh cooling (vs. €0.31/kWh electric chiller)

### Waste Stream
- Data center food waste (cafeterias): 30-50 tons/year
- Packaging waste (server equipment): 50-100 tons/year
- Regional agricultural residues: 500+ tons/year supply

### Value Proposition
> "Cut cooling costs 70%, achieve PUE 1.15, and turn your waste into 24/7 cooling. 7-month payback, then €5.4M annual savings."

### Entry Strategy
1. **Pilot:** 1-2 units at Google/Microsoft facility (CSR budget)
2. **Validation:** 6-month paid demonstration
3. **Scale:** 50-unit portfolio across 5-10 data centers
4. **Exit:** Sell to infrastructure PE (0.6x contract value)

### Priority Targets
| Company | Contact | Opportunity |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Google (European DCs) | Sustainability team | €300M contract potential |
| Microsoft (Nordic) | Energy team | €200M contract potential |
| Equinix | Operations | €150M colocation cooling |

## Business Case 3: 150 DIGESTER — BIOGAS PLANTS

**Campaign Name:** Project DIGEST+  
**Tagline:** *"Extract More from Your Digestate"*

### Target Profile
**Primary:** Existing biogas plants (anaerobic digesters)  
**Secondary:** Sewage treatment plants with AD  
**Geography:** Germany (9,500 plants), France, US, Brazil

### The Problem
- Biogas plants capture only 60-70% of feedstock energy
- 30-40% remains in digestate (currently land-applied or disposed)
- Digestate disposal costs: €20-50/ton
- Aging plants need efficiency upgrades

### Zero-X Solution
**Configuration:** 150 Digester (X-150 + methanization)
- **Input:** Biogas digestate/sludge
- **Output:** High-methane gas (85-95%) for pipeline injection
- **Efficiency gain:** +15-25% total methane capture

### Unit Economics (500 kW Biogas Plant)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Digestate processed** | 5,000 tons/year |
| **Additional methane** | +20% capture |
| **Extra revenue (RNG)** | €80,000/year |
| **Disposal savings** | €100,000/year |
| **CapEx** | €450,000 |
| **Payback** | 2.25 years |

### Contract Structure
- **Equipment sale:** €450K (one-time)
- **Service contract:** €30K/year (maintenance)
- **Revenue share:** Optional (10% of extra methane revenue)

### Waste Stream
- Anaerobic digestate (already collected)
- Post-digestion solids (high energy content)

### Value Proposition
> "Increase your biogas plant efficiency by 20% with post-treatment. Extract more methane from digestate, reduce disposal costs, and generate additional RNG revenue."

### Entry Strategy
1. **Partnership:** OEM deal with PlanET or EnviTec (German market leaders)
2. **Pilot:** 2-3 installations at partner sites
3. **Integration:** X-150 offered as "efficiency upgrade" module
4. **Scale:** 500+ retrofits in Germany alone
