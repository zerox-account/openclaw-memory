### 🇪🇹 Ethiopia - LONG-TERM POTENTIAL

**Market Overview:**
- **Massive population:** 120M+ (2nd largest in Africa)
- **Low electrification:** ~50% national, <10% rural
- **State-dominated:** Ethiopian Electric Power monopoly
- **Reform underway:** Gradual privatization opening

**Opportunities:**
1. **Coffee processing:** Major export sector, biomass waste
2. **Flower farms:** Energy-intensive, near Addis
3. **Refugee camps:** 800K+ refugees (GIZ ESDS angle)

**Challenges:**
- Currency convertibility issues
- Bureaucratic processes
- Political instability (North)

**Recommended Entry:**
- Wait for regulatory clarity
- Partner with development agencies (GIZ already active)
- Focus on humanitarian/Refugee camp pilots first
