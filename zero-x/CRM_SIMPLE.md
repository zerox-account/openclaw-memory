## 🔄 WORKFLOW STATUSES

### Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning | Next Action |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **NEW** | Contact added, no outreach yet | Send initial email |
| **CONTACTED** | Initial email sent | Wait for response (7 days) |
| **FOLLOW_UP** | No response to initial | Send follow-up email |
| **RESPONDED** | Contact replied | Schedule call/send info |
| **MEETING_SCHEDULED** | Call/meeting planned | Prepare for meeting |
| **MEETING_HELD** | Call/meeting completed | Send follow-up/next steps |
| **DEAD** | No interest/wrong contact | Archive |
| **CONVERTED** | Active collaboration | Manage relationship |

### Follow-up Schedule

| Stage | Timing | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Initial Contact | Day 0 | Send email |
| Follow-up #1 | Day 7 | Send follow-up if no response |
| Follow-up #2 | Day 14 | Send final follow-up |
| Archive | Day 21 | Mark as DEAD if no response |
