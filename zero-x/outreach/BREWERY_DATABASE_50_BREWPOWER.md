## Executive Summary: Brewery Opportunity

**The Waste Problem:**
- **37 million tonnes** of Brewers' Spent Grain (BSG) generated globally per year
- **20 kg BSG** produced for every 100L of beer brewed
- **85% of brewing waste** is spent grain
- Landfill cost: €50-100/ton + environmental liability

**The Solution:**
- X-150 converts BSG to 150kW electricity + 300kW thermal
- Byproducts: Biochar fertilizer + distilled water
- **3-year ROI** validated (Hepworth Brewery UK, Bavarian brewery)
- **99.8% waste diversion** achievable

**Market Size:**
- Global breweries: 10,000+ craft + 500+ major
- Top targets: Heineken, AB InBev, Carlsberg, Molson Coors
- Contract value: €60M+ global portfolio
