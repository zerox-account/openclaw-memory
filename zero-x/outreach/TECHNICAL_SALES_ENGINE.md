## Phase 3: Automation Architecture

### Tech Stack (Small Team, High Output)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost | Integration |
|------|---------|------|-------------|
| **Apollo.io** | Contact data, email sequences | $59/mo | Primary outreach |
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | Social selling, InMail | $80/mo | Parallel channel |
| **Calendly** | Meeting booking | $12/mo | Friction removal |
| **HubSpot CRM** | Pipeline management | Free tier | Central tracking |
| **Loom** | Personal video messages | Free | Tier A differentiation |
| **ChatGPT API** | Personalization at scale | Usage-based | Message variation |

### Automated Workflow

```
Day 0: Import contact → Enrich data (Apollo) → Segment (Tier A/B/C)
Day 1: Tier A: Personal video (Loom) + LinkedIn connection
       Tier B: Personalized email (AI-assisted) + LinkedIn
       Tier C: Automated sequence entry
Day 3: No response → LinkedIn InMail (different angle)
Day 7: No response → Value-add content (case study, video)
Day 14: No response → Final breakup email + nurture list
Day 30: Re-engagement with new content/announcement
```

### Response Handling

| Response Type | Action | SLA | Owner |
|---------------|--------|-----|-------|
| **Technical question** | Schedule 15-min technical call | 24 hrs | Clemens/Engineer |
| **Pilot interest** | Send NDA + preliminary proposal | 48 hrs | Sales Lead |
| **Budget/timeline question** | Discovery call + qualification | 24 hrs | Sales Lead |
| **Partnership inquiry** | Executive intro call | 48 hrs | Julien/Clemens |
| **Not now** | Nurture sequence (quarterly updates) | Auto | Marketing |
