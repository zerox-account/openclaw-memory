## M&A Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Outreach (Months 1-2)
- **Targets:** Tier 1 Strategic Acquirers (Covanta, Siemens, Cummins, Caterpillar)
- **Approach:** Partnership discussions → technology validation → acquisition interest
- **Goal:** 3-5 NDAs signed, preliminary interest gauged

### Phase 2: Financial Buyer Engagement (Months 2-3)
- **Targets:** KKR, ECP, Brookfield, Macquarie
- **Approach:** Data room preparation, management presentations
- **Goal:** Term sheet discussions, valuation benchmarking

### Phase 3: Competitive Process (Months 3-6)
- **Approach:** Formal sale process, management presentations, site visits
- **Goal:** Multiple bids, optimize valuation

### Phase 4: Transaction Close (Months 6-12)
- **Approach:** Due diligence, definitive agreement, closing
- **Goal:** Optimal valuation and terms
