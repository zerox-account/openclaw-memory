## Campaign Overview

**The VAULT Africa Initiative** deploys containerized biomass gasification units that convert agricultural and biowaste into base-load power, replacing expensive diesel generators while producing biochar fertilizer and distilled water.

**Partnership Path:** African universities → German development agencies → Local governments → Replicable pilot projects

**Key Value Propositions:**
- Replace diesel (save 50-60% on energy costs)
- Create local jobs (10+ per installation)
- Generate carbon credits
- Produce biochar fertilizer
- Distilled water as byproduct
