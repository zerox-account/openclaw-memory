## PROTOCOLS FOR GOVERNMENT ENGAGEMENT

### Initial Contact
1. **LinkedIn InMail first** (less intrusive than email)
2. **Reference mutual connections** if any
3. **Lead with credibility:** Paris validation, Fraunhofer partnership
4. **Specific ask:** Pilot program, not vague "partnership"

### Follow-up
1. **Wait 7 days** before follow-up
2. **Offer value first:** Technical brief, site visit invitation
3. **Copy deputy/assistant** if no response (they often manage calendar)

### Meeting Request
1. **Propose specific dates/times** (reduces back-and-forth)
2. **Offer to travel** to their office (shows commitment)
3. **Bring technical expert** (Clemens) + business lead (Hans/Julien)

### Documentation
1. **Log all contacts in CRM** (HubSpot)
2. **Record approval authority** (who can say yes to pilot)
3. **Track procurement timelines** (government budget cycles)
