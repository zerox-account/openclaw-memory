## Pitch Templates by Category

### For Clean Energy Publications

**Subject:** Paris-Validated Waste-to-Energy Tech Now Deploying to Africa

> PV Magazine,
>
> Zero-X has completed Project Cometha for the City of Paris — 116+ hours of validated operation converting waste to 650kW thermal power per unit.
>
> We're now scaling to rural Africa with containerized X-150 units that process 150kg/hour of organic waste into base-load power, hydrogen, or cooling. Think "solar hybrid meets waste management."
>
> The twist: SYNGAPURE catalyst achieves 94% tar removal, enabling multi-output configurations (electricity, heat, hydrogen, SAF, RNG) from the same unit.
>
> Available for interview: Clemens [Zero-X Lead], live from Brandenburg R&D facility.
>
> Visuals: Paris operation data, container deployment time-lapse, 6 output configurations.

### For Waste Management Publications

**Subject:** Municipal Waste-to-Energy That Actually Works — Paris Proves It

> Waste Dive,
>
> While anaerobic digestion struggles with efficiency, Zero-X's thermal gasification achieves 86% system efficiency and 650kW thermal output from containerized units.
>
> Project Cometha (Paris) validation by Fraunhofer IKTS proves technical viability. Now targeting Lagos, Nairobi, and Dar es Salaam municipal waste streams.
>
> Key differentiator: 6-7 week deployment vs. 2+ years for traditional WtE plants.
>
> Story angles:
> - Why thermal gasification beats anaerobic digestion for small-scale
> - Municipal PPP models (Nigeria pilot)
> - Landfill diversion economics

### For African Business Publications

**Subject:** German Climate Tech + African Agent Network = 1,000 Green Jobs

> TechCabal,
>
> Zero-X is deploying X-150 waste-to-energy units across Africa using a "distributor partner" model that creates local employment.
>
> Each unit = 15,000 people electrified + 5-10 local jobs (installation, service, feedstock supply).
>
> First markets: Nigeria (REA partnership), Kenya (Powerhive collaboration), Tanzania (ministry pilot).
>
> The team: Former Cometha engineers (Paris) + local distributors + government partnerships.
>
> Exclusive: Financial model and job creation data available under NDA.

### For Climate Publications

**Subject:** Carbon-Negative Rural Electrification — The Math Works

> Climate Home News,

> Each X-150 unit:
> - Processes 1,000 tonnes/year of organic waste
> - Avoids 2,000+ tonnes CO2 (landfill + diesel displacement)
