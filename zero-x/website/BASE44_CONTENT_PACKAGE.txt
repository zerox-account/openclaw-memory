## TECHNOLOGY SECTION

TITLE:
X-150 Modular Gasification System

CONTENT:
The X-150 is a containerized, single-stage downdraft gasification system with integrated tar reformer, optimized for high-ash multifuel operations and flexible for CHP operation with air or oxy-steam process.

SPECS:
• Thermal Output: ~650kW
• Efficiency: 86%
• Footprint: Standard 40ft container
• Feedstock: Wood, sewage sludge, agricultural waste, MSW
• Validation: 1,000+ operating hours (Cométha Project)
