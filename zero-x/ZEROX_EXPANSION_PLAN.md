## 1. TECHNOLOGY OVERVIEW

### Core Product: X-150 Modular Gasification System

**What It Is:**
Containerized waste gasification unit that converts biomass and organic waste into syngas for power generation, heating, or cooling.

**Key Technical Specifications:**
- **Feedstock Capacity:** 150 kg/hour mixed waste
- **Ash Tolerance:** Up to 50% (vs. industry standard <5%)
- **Gasification Efficiency:** 86%
- **Thermal Output:** 550 kWth per unit
- **Dimensions:** 2.5m × 2.5m × 3m (shipping container footprint)
- **Weight:** 8 tons
- **Validated Hours:** 1,000+ (COMETHA facility, Paris)

**Competitive Moat:**
- Only containerized system processing high-ash mixed waste (agricultural residues, municipal waste, food waste)
- Competitors require clean, processed wood (<5% ash)
- SYNGAPURE catalyst achieves 94% tar removal
- 6-7 week deployment vs. 2+ years for traditional plants

## 9. COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Ash tolerance:** 50% vs. <5% (competitors)
2. **Feedstock flexibility:** Mixed waste vs. clean wood only
3. **Containerized:** 6-7 weeks deployment vs. 2+ years
4. **Validated:** 1,000+ hours vs. theoretical
5. **Multi-output:** Power + heat + biochar + water vs. power only

**Target Customers:**
- Currently using diesel generators (paying $0.35-0.60/kWh)
- Have waste disposal costs (negative fuel cost opportunity)
- Need 24/7 reliability (hospitals, data centers, mining)
- ESG mandates (carbon reduction requirements)

**Competitive Response:**
- **If solar companies enter:** Position as complementary (solar + storage can't provide base-load)
- **If conventional gasifiers improve:** Ash tolerance gap is fundamental physics advantage
- **If diesel prices drop:** Focus on ESG/carbon mandates and energy independence
