# Master Sales Agent - Step-by-Step Guide

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Weeks 1-2)

### Step 1: Know Your Product Inside Out
- [ ] Study every feature of X-150 system
- [ ] Memorize technical specs (150kW elec, 350kW thermal)
- [ ] Understand multi-fuel capability
- [ ] Know reference projects (Cométha, MFC, Equation Labs)
- [ ] Master the value proposition

### Step 2: Define Your Ideal Customer
- [ ] Identify target industries (mining, agriculture, data centers)
- [ ] Create customer personas
- [ ] Understand their pain points
- [ ] Map decision-making process
- [ ] Know their budget cycles

### Step 3: Build Your Toolkit
- [ ] Create email templates (German & English)
- [ ] Prepare pitch deck
- [ ] Develop case studies
- [ ] Build ROI calculator
- [ ] Set up CRM system

---

## PHASE 2: PROSPECTING (Weeks 3-4)

### Step 4: Research & List Building
- [ ] Use Perplexity to find target companies
- [ ] Identify decision-makers (CEO, CTO, Sustainability Director)
- [ ] Find contact information (LinkedIn, company websites)
- [ ] Validate emails via ZeroBounce
- [ ] Build segmented contact lists

### Step 5: Prioritize Your Pipeline
- [ ] Score leads (High/Medium/Low priority)
- [ ] Focus on high-value opportunities first
- [ ] Identify quick wins vs. long-term deals
- [ ] Set daily outreach targets (20-50 contacts)

---

## PHASE 3: OUTREACH (Ongoing)

### Step 6: Craft Compelling Messages
**Email Structure:**
1. **Subject Line:** Specific & urgent
2. **Opening:** Personalize with research
3. **Value Prop:** How you solve their problem
4. **Proof:** Case studies & references
5. **CTA:** Clear next step

**Example:**
```
Subject: Biomass CHP for [Company] - 40% energy cost reduction

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is expanding operations in [Region]. 

Our X-150 system could reduce your energy costs by 40% using local biomass waste.

Reference: Project Cométha - 2,500+ operational hours

Worth a 15-minute call?

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Step 7: Multi-Channel Approach
- [ ] Email (primary)
- [ ] LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Phone calls (follow-up)
- [ ] Industry events
- [ ] Referrals

### Step 8: Follow-Up System
**Day 0:** Initial email
**Day 3:** Follow-up #1 (different angle)
**Day 7:** Follow-up #2 (add value)
**Day 14:** Follow-up #3 (break-up email)
**Day 30:** Re-engage with news/insight

---

## PHASE 4: MEETINGS & CLOSING (Ongoing)

### Step 9: Discovery Call
**Before:**
- [ ] Research company thoroughly
- [ ] Prepare 10 questions
- [ ] Set agenda

**During:**
- [ ] Ask about their challenges
- [ ] Listen 70%, talk 30%
- [ ] Identify budget & timeline
- [ ] Find decision-makers
- [ ] Take detailed notes

**After:**
- [ ] Send recap email within 24h
- [ ] Share relevant materials
- [ ] Schedule next steps

### Step 10: Proposal & Negotiation
- [ ] Customize proposal to their needs
- [ ] Include ROI calculation
- [ ] Offer pilot project option
- [ ] Prepare for objections
- [ ] Know your walk-away price

**Common Objections & Responses:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Let's calculate your 3-year savings..." |
| "Not now" | "When would be better? What's the trigger?" |
| "Need approval" | "Who else should be in our next call?" |
| "Competitor cheaper" | "Let's compare total cost of ownership..." |

### Step 11: Close the Deal
- [ ] Ask for the business directly
- [ ] Create urgency (limited availability)
- [ ] Offer incentives (early adopter discount)
- [ ] Get signature
- [ ] Celebrate & document

## MINDSET OF A MASTER SALES AGENT

✅ **Hungry** - Always prospecting
✅ **Resilient** - Rejection is part of the game
✅ **Curious** - Always learning about customers
✅ **Disciplined** - Follow the process daily
✅ **Confident** - Believe in your product
✅ **Helpful** - Solve problems, don't sell features
✅ **Persistent** - Follow up until you get a yes or no
✅ **Organized** - CRM is your best friend
