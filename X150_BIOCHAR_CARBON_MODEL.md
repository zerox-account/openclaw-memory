## 1. Puro.earth Certification Pathway

### 1.1 Overview
Puro.earth is the world's first standard focused solely on carbon removal with durable storage (100+ years). The biochar methodology was the first carbon removal crediting methodology brought to market in 2019 and was updated in 2022/2025 to include the latest research.

### 1.2 Certification Requirements

#### Feedstock Eligibility
- **Accepted:** Biomass, biowaste, wood residues, agricultural residues
- **Preferred:** FSC/SFI certified or sustainably sourced residues
- **X-150 Fit:** Wood chips, agricultural residues, energy crops

#### Production Requirements
| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| Process | Pyrolysis (thermochemical conversion without oxygen) |
| Temperature | 350-700°C typical |
| Carbon Stability | Must demonstrate 100+ year durability |
| H:C Ratio | <0.7 (indicates stability) |
| O:C Ratio | <0.4 (indicates stability) |

#### Verification Process
1. **Lifecycle Assessment (LCA)** - Document net negativity
2. **Independent Audit** - Third-party validation & verification
3. **Facility Inspection** - On-site verification by accredited body
4. **CORC Issuance** - CO₂ Removal Certificates issued in Puro Registry
5. **Ongoing Monitoring** - Periodic re-verification required

### 1.3 Timeline to Certification

## 2. Carbon Credit Calculation

### 2.1 Biochar Production Assumptions

| Parameter | Value | Source/Justification |
|-----------|-------|---------------------|
| Biomass Input | 5,000 t/year | X-150 system capacity |
| Biochar Yield | 25% | Industry standard for slow pyrolysis |
| Biochar Output | 1,250 t/year | 5,000 × 0.25 |
| Fixed Carbon Content | 75% | Conservative for woody biomass |
| Carbon in Biochar | 937.5 t C/year | 1,250 × 0.75 |

### 2.2 Carbon Sequestration Calculation

```
Gross CO₂ Removal = Carbon in Biochar × (44/12)
                  = 937.5 tonnes C × 3.67
                  = 3,441 tonnes CO₂/year

Net CO₂ Removal (after process emissions deduction):
- Process energy (pyrolysis): ~150 tonnes CO₂eq
- Transport & handling: ~100 tonnes CO₂eq
- Other lifecycle emissions: ~150 tonnes CO₂eq
- Total deductions: ~400 tonnes CO₂eq

NET CO₂ REMOVAL = 3,441 - 400 = ~3,041 tonnes CO₂/year

Conservative estimate (Puro methodology): ~2,750 tonnes CO₂/year
```
