## Optimization Learnings

### What Maximizes Returns
1. **Negative fuel costs** (gate fees) - biggest impact
2. **High power prices** (diesel islands, industrial)
3. **Multiple revenue streams** (electricity + heat + biochar)
4. **High capacity factor** (>90% operating hours)
5. **Carbon credit revenue** (Puro.earth for biochar)

### What Destroys Returns
1. **Expensive biomass transport** (>50km)
2. **Low power prices** (<€0.10/kWh)
3. **Intermittent demand** (<70% capacity factor)
4. **High fuel costs** (>€150/tonne)
5. **Regulatory uncertainty** (permit delays)

### Sweet Spot Configurations

**Best Overall:**
- Waste feedstock with gate fees
- Industrial heat demand
- Island or weak grid location
- Carbon credit monetization

**Best for Speed:**
- Wood pellets (reliable supply)
- Grid-connected power
- Standard configuration
- Proven offtake

**Best for Scale:**
- Multiple units (portfolio)
- District heating network
- Long-term PPA
- Green bond financing

## Bankability Insights

### What Lenders Want to See
1. **Technology track record** - 1,000+ hours proven
2. **Fuel supply security** - contract or abundant local source
3. **Creditworthy offtaker** - investment grade or guarantee
4. **Experienced operator** - O&M contract or in-house
5. **Appropriate DSCR** - 1.25x minimum average

### Common Deal Breakers
- Single revenue stream
- Unproven fuel source
- Weak counterparty
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Excessive leverage (>80% debt)

### Financing Structures That Work

**Project Finance (70/30 debt/equity):**
- DSCR: 1.25-1.40x
- Tenor: 10-15 years
- Rate: 5-7%
- Best for: Large projects, strong offtake

**Development Finance (50/50 with grant):**
- Concessional rate: 2-4%
- Long tenor: 15-20 years
- Grant component: 20-40%
- Best for: Emerging markets, first-of-a-kind

**Corporate Balance Sheet:**
- Full equity or corporate guarantee
- Faster execution
- Lower transaction costs
- Best for: Repeat deployments, portfolio

## Risk Factors & Mitigations

### Fuel Price Risk
**Risk:** Biomass price volatility
**Mitigation:** Long-term supply contracts, fuel blending, geographic diversification

### Technology Risk
**Risk:** Equipment failure, performance shortfall
**Mitigation:** Warranties, maintenance contracts, proven technology

### Offtake Risk
**Risk:** Counterparty default, demand reduction
**Mitigation:** Creditworthy customers, take-or-pay, diversified offtake

### Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** Permit delays, policy changes
**Mitigation:** Early engagement, grandfathering provisions, political risk insurance

### Currency Risk
**Risk:** EUR/local currency fluctuations
**Mitigation:** Local currency debt, hedging, natural hedges (local costs)
