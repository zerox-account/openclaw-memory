## Operating Principles

### Model Validation Checklist
- [ ] All inputs have sources or justifications
- [ ] Sensitivities tested (±20% on key variables)
- [ ] Break-even analysis completed
- [ ] DSCR > 1.25x in all years (debt scenarios)
- [ ] IRR exceeds hurdle rate (typically 12-15%)
- [ ] Payback period < 7 years

### Ranking Criteria (1-10 Scale)

**Score 9-10 (Exceptional):**
- IRR > 20%
- DSCR > 1.5x
- Payback < 4 years
- Multiple revenue streams
- Low fuel cost or gate fees
- Strong offtake agreements

**Score 7-8 (Strong):**
- IRR 15-20%
- DSCR 1.3-1.5x
- Payback 4-6 years
- Proven technology
- Reasonable fuel costs

**Score 5-6 (Acceptable):**
- IRR 12-15%
- DSCR 1.2-1.3x
- Payback 6-8 years
- May require guarantees

**Score 3-4 (Marginal):**
- IRR 8-12%
- DSCR 1.1-1.2x
- Payback 8-10 years
- High risk factors

**Score 1-2 (Poor):**
- IRR < 8%
- DSCR < 1.1x
- Payback > 10 years
- Not bankable without support

### Bankability Requirements

**For Commercial Debt:**
- Minimum DSCR: 1.25x (average), 1.15x (minimum year)
- Minimum LLCR: 1.35x
- Debt tenor: 10-15 years
- Interest rate: 4-8% (depending on market)

**For Development Finance:**
- Minimum DSCR: 1.15x
- Concessional rates: 2-4%
- Longer tenors: 15-20 years
- Grant/blended finance possible
