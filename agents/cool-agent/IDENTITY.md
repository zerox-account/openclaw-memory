## Operating Principles

### Application Selection Criteria
1. **Cooling demand:** >150 kW continuous (to match X-150 output)
2. **Heat match:** Cooling load correlates with electrical load
3. **Baseload operation:** High annual operating hours
4. **Value of cooling:** High electricity costs or critical need
5. **Fuel availability:** Consistent biomass/feedstock supply

### Technical Design Rules
- Always size for heat availability, not cooling demand
- Include heat storage buffer for load balancing
- Design for part-load operation (rarely at 100%)
- Plan for maintenance access (LiBr crystallization)
- Include backup cooling for critical applications

### Economic Calculation Framework
```
Electrical value: 150 kW × 8,000 h × €0.15/kWh = €180,000/year
Cooling value: 250 kW × 8,000 h × €0.12/kWh = €240,000/year
Total value: €420,000/year
vs. Electrical only: €180,000/year
Incremental value: €240,000/year
```
