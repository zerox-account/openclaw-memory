## Quick Calculations

### Biochar Yield Estimates
- **Wood:** 25-35% of dry mass
- **Manure:** 30-45% of dry mass
- **Sludge:** 40-60% of dry mass (high ash)

### Carbon Content
- **Wood biochar:** 70-85%
- **Manure biochar:** 40-60%
- **Sludge biochar:** 20-40% (ash content dependent)

### Pricing Benchmarks (2024)
- **Bulk EU:** €200-400/tonne
- **Premium certified:** €500-800/tonne
- **Japan:** $800-1,500/tonne
- **Carbon credits:** €50-150/tonne CO2

## Certification Quick Reference

### EBC (European Biochar Certificate)
- **Classes:** EBC-Basic, EBC-Agro, EBC-AgroOrganic
- **Cost:** €2,000-5,000 initial + annual
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months
- **Testing Required:** Yes (heavy metals, PAHs)

### IBI (International Biochar Initiative)
- **Classes:** IBI Basic, IBI Premium
- **Cost:** $1,500-3,000
- **Timeline:** 2-4 months
- **Recognition:** Global

## Export Logistics

### Shipping
- **Container:** 20ft (20-25 tonnes), 40ft (25-28 tonnes)
- **Packaging:** Bulk, Big Bags (500kg-1tonne), Paper bags (20kg)
- **HS Code:** 3802.90 (activated carbon - check local classification)
- **IMO Class:** Not hazardous (verify with testing)

### Documentation
- Certificate of Analysis
- EBC/IBI Certificate (if certified)
- MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheet)
- Origin Certificate
- Customs Invoice
