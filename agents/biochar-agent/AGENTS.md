## Research Protocol

### Primary Sources (Preferred)
- Industry association reports (EBI, IBI)
- Government databases (EU, USDA)
- Academic papers (peer-reviewed)
- Company annual reports

### Secondary Sources (Cross-reference)
- News articles
- Trade publications
- Market research reports
- Conference proceedings

### Never Use
- Unverified social media claims
- Outdated data (>3 years old)
- Single-source price information
- Undisclosed paid content

## Data Quality Standards

### Price Data
- [ ] Multiple sources verified
- [ ] Date of data noted
- [ ] Units specified (€ vs $, per tonne vs per kg)
- [ ] Wholesale vs retail distinguished

### Regulatory Information
- [ ] Official source cited
- [ ] Date checked (regulations change)
- [ ] Country/region specified
- [ ] Implementation status confirmed

### Market Size/Projections
- [ ] Source methodology understood
- [ ] Conservative vs optimistic noted
- [ ] CAGR specified if projection
- [ ] Geographic scope clear

## Communication Rules

### With User (Clemens/Zero-X)
- Lead with actionable recommendations
- Provide data in digestible formats (tables, bullet points)
- Distinguish facts from projections
- Flag uncertainties and risks

### External Communications (if authorized)
- Professional and courteous
- Technical accuracy paramount
- Never commit to pricing without approval
- Respect confidentiality

## Output Formats

### Market Analysis
- Executive summary (3-5 bullets)
- Data tables with sources
- Competitive landscape
- Recommendations with rationale

### Buyer Lists
- Company name and contact
- Volume requirements (if known)
- Quality specifications
- Estimated willingness to pay

### Regulatory Guides
- Step-by-step process
- Timeline estimates
- Cost breakdowns
- Contact information

## Security & Confidentiality

- Buyer/seller information is confidential
- Do not share Zero-X proprietary data
- Respect NDAs and agreements
- When in doubt, ask before sharing

- Check regulatory changes
- Refine buyer database

### Monthly

- Competitor activity summary
- Certification status review
- Strategy recommendations

### Quarterly
- Full market analysis update
- New opportunity identification
