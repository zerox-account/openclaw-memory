# AGENTS.md - Agent Research Workspace Rules

## First Run

If `BOOTSTRAP.md` exists, follow initialization, then delete.

## Every Session

Before starting work:

1. **Read SOUL.md** - Who you are (research partnership expert)
2. **Read IDENTITY.md** - Your methodology and expertise
3. **Read USER.md** - Zero-X context and goals
4. **Read memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md** - Today's activities
5. **Read MEMORY.md** - Partner database and campaign history

## Research Protocol

### Professor Profiling (Before Contact)

**Required Research:**
- [ ] Recent publications (last 3 years, top 5)
- [ ] Current research projects
- [ ] Department and institutional context
- [ ] Previous industry partnerships
- [ ] Student supervision activity

**Sources (in order of priority):**
1. Google Scholar (publications, citations)
2. University website (official profile)
3. ResearchGate (recent activity)
4. LinkedIn (professional background)
5. ORCID (comprehensive publication list)

### Email Preparation Checklist

**Before Writing:**
- [ ] Professor research completed
- [ ] Specific connection identified
- [ ] Value proposition clear
- [ ] Correct title verified
- [ ] Email validated (ZeroBounce)

**Before Sending:**
- [ ] User approval obtained (explicit "SEND")
- [ ] Email proofread
- [ ] Attachments ready (if any)
- [ ] Log entry prepared

## Communication Standards

### Never Send Without
1. Specific reference to professor's work
2. Clear value proposition
3. Professional tone appropriate to culture
4. Validated email address
5. Explicit user approval

### Follow-up Rules
- Wait 7 days minimum before first follow-up
- Maximum 3 follow-ups total
- Each follow-up adds new information or value
- Final follow-up is polite closure

### Academic Etiquette
- Respect semester schedules (avoid exam periods)
- Acknowledge academic timelines (grants, publications)
- Don't rush decisions
- Appreciate that "no" is acceptable

## Data Management

### Contact Database Fields
- Full name
- Title and position
- Institution and department
- Email (validated)
- Research focus
- Contact history
- Status (NEW, CONTACTED, RESPONDED, etc.)
- Notes

### Campaign Tracking
- Date sent
- Template used
- Subject line
- Response date
- Response content
- Next action
- Assigned to

## Quality Standards

### Email Quality Check
- [ ] Personalized opening

- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Appropriate tone
- [ ] Professional signature
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors

### Research Quality Check

## Security & Ethics

- Never misrepresent Zero-X capabilities
- Don't promise funding that doesn't exist
- Respect confidentiality of professor communications
- Don't share contact lists externally
- Acknowledge data gaps honestly
