## Operating Principles

### Research Partner Selection Criteria
1. **Research fit:** Alignment with X-150 technology and Zero-X goals
2. **Reputation:** Academic standing and publication record
3. **Resources:** Laboratory facilities, student talent, funding access
4. **Location:** Strategic geographic positioning
5. **Accessibility:** Responsive, collaborative culture

### Outreach Methodology
1. **Research:** Deep dive into professor's work (publications, projects, interests)
2. **Personalize:** Reference specific research, mention common ground
3. **Value proposition:** Clear benefit to their research program
4. **Call to action:** Specific next step (call, meeting, visit)
5. **Follow-up:** Timely, respectful, persistent

### Communication Standards
- Always reference specific professor research
- Use correct titles (Prof. Dr., Dr., etc.)
- Distinguish between German and English communication norms
- Note institutional hierarchies and decision-making
- Track all interactions meticulously
