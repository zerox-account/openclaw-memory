# TOOLS.md - Agent Africa Tools & Commands

## Command Triggers

| Command | Action |
|---------|--------|
| `/country [name]` | Show electrification data for specific country |
| `/mini-grid` | List active mini-grid programs and opportunities |
| `/financing` | Show available funding sources for region |
| `/feedstock` | Analyze biomass availability in [country/region] |
| `/partners` | Identify potential local partners in [country] |
| `/regulation` | Check regulatory requirements for [country] |

## Country Quick Reference

### Ghana
- **Electrification:** 85% national, 67% rural
- **Regulator:** Energy Commission (energycom.gov.gh)
- **Utility:** ECG (electricity company)
- **Mini-grid policy:** Yes, licensed framework
- **Key feedstocks:** Palm waste, cocoa husks, rice straw
- **Currency:** GHS (Ghana Cedi)
- **Language:** English
- **Development partners:** GIZ, USAID, World Bank

### Nigeria
- **Electrification:** 55% national, 39% rural
- **Regulator:** NERC (nerc.gov.ng)
- **Utility:** PHCN (privatized distribution companies)
- **Mini-grid policy:** Yes, significant opportunity
- **Key feedstocks:** Rice husks, cassava waste, sawdust
- **Currency:** NGN (Naira)
- **Language:** English
- **Challenges:** Grid reliability, security in some regions

### Kenya
- **Electrification:** 75% national, 65% rural
- **Regulator:** EPRA (epra.go.ke)
- **Utility:** Kenya Power
- **Mini-grid policy:** Yes, leading in Africa
- **Key feedstocks:** Sugarcane bagasse, coffee husks, wood chips
- **Currency:** KES (Kenyan Shilling)
- **Language:** English, Swahili
- **Strengths:** Innovation hub, strong mobile money

### Tanzania
- **Electrification:** 40% national, 24% rural
- **Regulator:** EWURA (ewura.go.tz)
- **Utility:** TANESCO
- **Mini-grid policy:** Yes, SPP framework
- **Key feedstocks:** Sisal waste, rice husks, cashew shells
- **Currency:** TZS (Tanzanian Shilling)
- **Language:** Swahili, English

### Uganda
- **Electrification:** 42% national, 32% rural
- **Regulator:** ERA (era.or.ug)
- **Utility:** UMEME
- **Mini-grid policy:** Yes
- **Key feedstocks:** Coffee husks, maize cobs, wood waste
- **Currency:** UGX (Ugandan Shilling)
- **Language:** English, Swahili

## Financing Mechanisms

### Results-Based Financing (RBF)
- **World Bank:** Scaling Solar, Mini-Grid RBF
- **AfDB:** Sustainable Energy Fund for Africa (SEFA)
- **GIZ:** Green People's Energy
- **USAID:** Power Africa RBF programs

### Carbon Finance
- **Gold Standard:** Goldstandard.org
- **Verra (VCS):** verra.org
- **CDM (Clean Development Mechanism):** UNFCCC

### Local Currency Financing
- **Local banks:** Stanbic, Ecobank, Standard Chartered
- **Development finance institutions:** AfDB, IFC, FMO
- **Currency risk:** Major challenge for USD/EUR debt
