## Third Product Line: 150 Impact Cool
**Product:** Gasification + Absorption Chiller System  
**Mission:** Waste-to-Refrigeration for off-grid cooling

### Technical Specifications
- **Thermal Input:** 700 kW from X150 boiler
- **Cooling Output (by chiller type):**
  - Single-effect (COP 0.6): ~420 kW cooling
  - Double-effect (COP 1.0): ~700 kW cooling
  - Triple-effect (COP 1.5-1.8): ~1,050-1,260 kW cooling
- **Chiller CapEx:** ~$400-420k (integrated system)
- **Opex:** Minimal (<15 kW electrical for pumps/fans)
- **Key Advantage:** Heat-driven cooling, no electricity required

### Target Applications
- Rural hospitals (vaccine cold chain)
- Agricultural cold storage (post-harvest loss reduction)
- Data centers (free cooling from waste heat)
- Food processing (process cooling)

### Value Proposition
> "Turn waste into refrigeration. 700 kW of cooling with no electricity input."
