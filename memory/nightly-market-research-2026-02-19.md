## 1. Waste-to-Energy News

### Recent Developments (Past 48 Hours)

**Brazil: Major Biomethane Plant Financing**  
- **Source:** Bioenergy News, Feb 19, 2026  
- **Details:** Brazilian Development Bank (BNDES) approved R$148.5 million financing for Bioo Paraná Holding SA to build a biomethane plant in Toledo, western Paraná. Total project investment: R$196 million.
- **Capacity:** 11 million cubic metres of biomethane annually
- **Environmental Impact:** ~80 tonnes CO₂ equivalent prevented annually
- **Co-products:** Organic matrix fertiliser + food-grade biogenic CO₂ for industrial use
- **Strategic Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐ **HIGH** - Demonstrates the circular economy model integrating renewable energy, organic waste reuse, and high-value industrial inputs. The inclusion of food-grade CO₂ as a revenue stream is particularly interesting for Zero-X Labs' gasification projects.

**Italy: Eni Biorefinery Circular Economy Project**  
- **Source:** Bioenergy News, Feb 18, 2026  
- **Details:** Anaergia supplying anaerobic digestion technology to €50M project at Eni's Gela biorefinery in Sicily
- **Output:** >70,000 MWh renewable natural gas annually
- **Innovation:** High-viscosity digestion systems processing degumming soil (HVO by-product) with >80% recovery rate
- **Timeline:** Commissioning expected May 2027
- **Market Context:** 250+ HVO plants worldwide; market projected to grow 35% by 2030
- **Strategic Relevance:** ⭐⭐⭐ **HIGH** - Creates potential partnership opportunities for gasification technology integration with HVO production. The model could be replicated across Eni's wider biorefining portfolio.

**New York: Landmark Waste-to-Energy Contract Awarded**
