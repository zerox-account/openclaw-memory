## 🔬 CRITICAL TECHNICAL DATA - X-150 SEWAGE SLUDGE CLOSED-LOOP SYSTEM (From J Lo)

**Project: X-150 Sewage Sludge to Gas Closed-Loop System**

### System Architecture:
1. **Sewage sludge** converted to gas
2. **Gas burned in boiler**
3. **Boiler runs heater** (CCM proprietary "oil-bath dryer")
4. **Dried feedstock** fed into gasification chamber
5. **Closed-loop system** - no external energy required (depending on moisture/energy balance)
6. **Final output:** Biochar only

### Target Markets:
- Small communities: **10,000 - 20,000 people**
- Mixed-fuel use cases
- Waste treatment + compaction system

### Feedstock Flexibility (as demonstrated in Paris):
- ✅ Sewage sludge (primary)
- ✅ Municipal solid waste
- ✅ Horse manure
- ✅ Other biomass sources

**Benefit:** Blending inputs increases fuel volume + improves combustion quality → higher calorific value

### Project Reference:
- **Tested in:** Project Cométha (Paris)
- **Stable operation with:** Up to **50% ash content** fuel

---

### X-150 Core IP - Patented Crushing System:

**Problem Solved:**
- High mineral/ash content fuels melt at ~1,200°C reactor temperatures

**Solution:**
1. **Patented crushing system** breaks sludge clinkers at ~**400°C**
2. At 400°C: Material still **brittle** → less energy required for crushing
3. Crushed clinker removed with ash

**Two Ash Exit Paths:**
1. **Smaller particles:** Carried with gas stream → filtered via pulse filter
2. **Heavier particles:** Removed from reactor bottom

**Critical Mechanism:**
- Lower reactor layer (molten clinkers + biochar) = **activated carbon filter**
- Captures particles and pollutants
- Results in: **Clean exhaust air** with reduced pollutant content

### Key Differentiators:
- ✅ Self-sustaining (no external energy)
- ✅ Handles 50% ash content
- ✅ Patented low-temp clinker crushing
- ✅ Biochar acts as natural filter
- ✅ Only output: biochar (waste-to-resource)
- ✅ Scale: 10K-20K person communities
