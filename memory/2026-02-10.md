### 1. NEVER INVENT FACTS
- Made up "12 units Q2" - WRONG
- Consequence: Loss of trust, -50 points penalty
- Rule: If unknown, ask Clemens or leave out

### 2. NO AI PHRASES  
- Never: "I hope this message finds you well"
- Use direct, context-specific openings
- Example GOOD: "Following up on your introduction..."

### 3. CLEAR VALUE FOR RECIPIENT
- Show what THEY gain, not what you need
- Focus on recipient's benefit

### 4. SHORT AND CONCRETE
- First 2 sentences deliver value
- Bullet points for facts
- Max 5-6 sentences

### 5. NO ARTIFICIAL SCARCITY
- Don't invent "Only 2 slots left"
- Destroys credibility

### 6. RESPECTFUL TONE
- Professional + friendly + direct
- Not too salesy, not too cold

### 7. CHECK IMPORTANT EMAILS FIRST
- Wait for Clemens approval on critical emails
- Better slow and right than fast and wrong

### 8. USE VERIFIED FACTS ONLY
- From MEMORY.md, conversations, documents
- Unknown = ask or leave out
