# Ukraine Outreach Strategy - Decentralized Energy & Biomass
## Project: X-150 Modular Biomass CHP System

---

## 🎯 STRATEGIC CONTEXT

**Why Ukraine:**
- Critical energy infrastructure damage due to conflict
- High need for decentralized, resilient energy systems
- Abundant agricultural biomass/waste resources
- EU integration pathway → Horizon Europe eligibility
- International reconstruction funding available

**Key Challenges:**
- Security situation (avoid sensitive locations)
- Communication infrastructure disruptions
- Priorities: humanitarian aid > commercial deals
- Approach: Research partnership, NOT sales

---

## 🚨 SENSITIVITY GUIDELINES

### DO:
- ✅ Emphasize research partnership, NOT sales
- ✅ Offer knowledge transfer, training
- ✅ Mention EU/Horizon Europe funding possibilities
- ✅ Focus on post-war reconstruction context
- ✅ Use neutral, academic language
- ✅ Be patient with response times

### DON'T:
- ❌ Mention specific locations near conflict zones
- ❌ Suggest immediate deployment
- ❌ Use commercial/promotional language
- ❌ Ignore security situation
- ❌ Send unsolicited attachments
- ❌ Follow up aggressively
