# 🔒 FINANCIAL INFORMATION HANDLING PROTOCOL

**Applies To:** EX Venture Inc. and all subsidiaries (Zero-X, MFC/Energaia, Seraph AI)  
**Status:** MANDATORY — SEC Compliance Required  
**Effective:** Immediately

---

## NEVER Include Without EXPLICIT Written Approval

The following information is **RESTRICTED** and requires explicit written approval before inclusion in any communication:

- ❌ Payment amounts
- ❌ Grant funding figures
- ❌ Investment amounts
- ❌ Salary/compensation details
- ❌ Budget allocations
- ❌ Financial claims or pending payments
- ❌ Bank account details
- ❌ Tax-related figures

---

## MANDATORY RULES

### 1. Separate Channels
Financial discussions happen in **dedicated, secure channels only**.  
**Never mix** with operational tasks in shared workspaces or general communications.

### 2. Explicit Confirmation Required
If a user mentions an amount in conversation, **DO NOT** include it in emails or external communications unless they explicitly say:

> "Include [specific amount] in the email."

### 3. Ask First
If you're unsure whether to include financial info:  
🛑 **STOP.**  
Ask:
> "Should I include the specific amount, or keep this general?"

### 4. Secure Alternatives
When amounts **ARE** needed, suggest:
- Encrypted messaging (Signal, WhatsApp with disappearing messages)
- Phone call
- In-person discussion
- Secure document sharing (not email attachments)

### 5. SEC Compliance
**EX Venture Inc. is SEC registered.**  
Unintentional disclosure of financial information = **regulatory violation + legal liability**.

---

## CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION

- Regulatory fines
- Loss of investor trust
- Legal exposure
- Reputational damage

---

## GUIDING PRINCIPLE

> **WHEN IN DOUBT, LEAVE IT OUT.**

---

## Protocol Reference

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| User mentions "$50M raise" in chat | Do NOT include in emails/docs without explicit: "Include the $50M figure" |
| Drafting investor deck with financials | Use placeholders: "[REVENUE FIGURE]" or "Contact [CFO] for specifics" |
| Asked to share budget in group channel | Redirect to secure 1:1 or ask: "Should I share specific numbers or keep general?" |
| Unclear if amount is sensitive | Assume it is. Ask first. |
